Doctors About Corona Virus

What doctors say..

doctors advise wearing masks whenever you're around others to stop the virus from spreading through the air. They say it's crucial to keep some distance from people outside your household to lower the risk of getting infected. Washing your hands often with soap and water or using hand sanitizer can kill any germs you might have picked up. Avoiding large gatherings where lots of people are close together can help prevent the virus from spreading quickly. And finally, they suggest getting vaccinated when you can to protect yourself and others from getting seriously sick with COVID-19.
